## Format rules

- **Color tags per active engagement:** 🟦 / 🟨 / 🟩 (3 active = 3 colors). 🟪 reserved for workstreams. Order accounts by Monday time-urgency (most-urgent first), not alphabetical or priority-stack order.
- **Each top-3 item:** action verb + object in bold, then 1-2 sentences of context with verbatim quotes / source citations.
- **Skip paused / shadow engagements entirely** — no top-3, no section. Only surface in the day-shape line if there's a meeting that day (e.g., a C&W meeting Harper observes).
- **Workstreams = top 3 across, not per-workstream.** If fewer than 3 workstreams have Monday-actionable items, surface what you have and skip the rest. Don't pad.
- **Voice:** same as the vault prep file. Direct, terse, operator-grade. No filler.
- **No em dashes anywhere** (per Harper's global preferences). Hyphens or rewrites only.
