## Format rules

- **Color tags:** 🟦 GEV / 🟨 Syneos / 🟩 KKR. Fixed assignment, not order-dependent.
- **Order:** by SFDC close-date proximity, most-urgent first. GEV typically leads (5/29 close target) unless another deal has moved closer.
- **Read-only.** This DM never modifies MEDDPICC. If scoring is stale, the DM surfaces that in Sources and Harper decides whether to refresh.
- **Skip paused / shadow.** No HP, no C&W. If they appear on the day-shape calendar, surface in the day-shape line only.
- **No em dashes** (per Harper's global preferences). Hyphens or rewrites only.
- **Voice:** matches vault file — operator-grade, terse. Phone-readable in 60 seconds.
- **Delta arrows:** ↑ aggregate improved (more 🟩, fewer 🟥), ↓ worsened, → no change.
